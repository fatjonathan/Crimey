Prop 57: Inmates who are not condemned or sentenced to life without the possibility of parole may qualify for amplified good conduct credits pursuant to Article I, § 32 of the California Constitution, which authorized the Department of Corrections and Rehabilitation to award credits for good behavior beyond what is authorized by statute. Good conduct credits are awarded to eligible inmates who comply with all rules within a prison and perform the duties as assigned on a regular basis. Eligibility is determined by CDCR and is subject to change without notice.
